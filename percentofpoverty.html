Luzon

The largest and most populous island in the Philippines, Luzon accounts for a significant portion of the country's economy. However, poverty remains a pressing issue, especially in rural areas. In recent years, poverty rates in Luzon have been gradually declining, but regional disparities persist.

Poverty Rate: Approximately 13.4% (as per the latest data from the Philippine Statistics Authority, 2021).

Mindanao

Mindanao, the southernmost major island group, experiences the highest poverty rates in the Philippines. This is partly due to historical conflicts, lack of infrastructure, and limited access to education and healthcare. Despite efforts to improve conditions, Mindanao remains the poorest region in the country.

Poverty Rate: Approximately 31.1% (latest data from the Philippine Statistics Authority, 2021).
